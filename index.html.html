How to read the results

  • Basic Net — pure house math: equity recovered minus interest, taxes, insurance, maintenance, HOA, and selling costs.
  • Advanced Net — adds rent you avoided and any tax benefit, then subtracts opportunity cost on your upfront cash.
  • Break-even is the first month where the respective Net turns positive.
v2 formula fix: Basic Net now subtracts interest paid only (not total P+I). Principal paydown is already captured in Cash Back From Sale, so counting it again in "Mortgage Paid" was inflating true costs. Opportunity cost default raised to 6% (S&P long-run avg) for a more realistic comparison.
